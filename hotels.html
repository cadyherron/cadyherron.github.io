RESERVED ROOMS

HOLIDAY INN EXPRESS - Located in Mill Valley, 8.2 miles from the wedding location. We've reserved a group rate of $209-229 per night, which includes breakfast, parking, pool and gym access, s'mores in the evening, and 2 hours of bike rentals. You're welcome to book for the wedding weekend only, or for additional days at the same rate. Please click here for the reservations flyer, which includes instructions on how to book. Rooms must be reserved before August 7, 2015.

ACQUA HOTEL - Also located in Mill Valley, 8.9 miles from the wedding location. We've reserved a group rate of 10% off of list price, which will be around $215 per night. This preferred rate is available for any days between August 30 - September 11, and it also includes breakfast, parking, and 2 hours of bike rentals. The minimum length of stay at Acqua is two nights. Please click here for the reservations flyer, which includes instructions on how to book. There is no cutoff date for booking your room.
