WHAT SHOULD I WEAR? The ceremony will be held indoors, and the temperature should be pretty comfortable. The cocktail hour will be held outside, so we suggest bringing a jacket or a scarf. The best description for attire is probably "cocktail". Greg and Julia will be pretty dressed up - please feel free to join us!

WHAT TIME SHOULD I ARRIVE? The Marin Headlands is a popular weekend destination, and there is often traffic for the one-way tunnel that leads out to our wedding. There is also an art festival the same day as our wedding, which could cause additional traffic. We recommend arriving early, around 2pm - that way you can relax and explore nearby Rodeo Beach. If you're coming from one of the Mill Valley hotels, you should allow 40 minutes for the drive.

WHAT WILL I BE EATING? Delicious, local food will be provided by Katie Powers Catering. Please let us know if you have any dietary restrictions. We've chosen some great wine, beer, and champagne to be enjoyed throughout the night.

WHAT SHOULD I DO WHILE I'M IN TOWN? Here are a few of our favorite spots:

EATS - Sol Food (Mill Valley and San Rafael), Hog Island Oyster Co (Tomales Bay and SF), Tartine Bakery (SF), Four Barrel Coffee (SF), Outerlands (SF)

HIKING - Ring Mountain (Corte Madera), Muir Woods (Mill Valley), Phoenix Lake (Kentfield), Tennessee Valley (Mill Valley), Corona Heights (SF)

BEACHES - Muir Beach, Bolinas Beach, Stinson Beach
